How it Works

You’re 4 steps away from migrating to a new service.

1

Select your source and destination

Select the source platform/service from where you have your music, playlist album etc saved and also the destination platform/source where you want to transfer your media assets too.

2

Select the item you want to transfer

Select the items you will like to transfer, do you want to transfer only tracks, album, artist or all media assets. take charge of what you will like to transfer at a go from one service to another.

3

Confirm the transfer

Confirm the transfer by agreeing to give us permission to access and make changes to your streaming platforms or services account . This permission will enable us to carry out the migration seamlessly.

4

Transfer

Initiate your transfer and wait for your media assets to fully migrate from your source service/platform to your designated service or platform within 20 seconds from the time of initiation.
